Admission

Join St. Michael FSJ School for an enriching educational experience. Admission is open, and we welcome students who are eager to learn and grow.

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to ensure a smooth transition for new students. Here are the key steps:

  1. Submit the admission application form Online or at the school office.
  2. Attend an admission interview with our friendly staff.
  3. Complete the required admission paperwork.
  4. Receive admission confirmation and join the St. Michael FSJ School community.

Admission Requirements

We have a few simple requirements for admission:

  • Proof of age (birth certificate or passport)
  • Previous academic records (if applicable)
  • Completed application form
  • Parent/guardian contact information
