Extracurricular Activities

Engage your child in a variety of extracurricular activities that complement academic learning and promote the overall development of our learners.

Featured Programs

Art Club

Let your child explore his/her creative side with our Art Club, where learners can express themselves through various art forms. Regular art exhibitions showcase the talent of our budding artists.

Sports Team

Learners are encouraged to join our sports teams and participate in exciting competitions, fostering teamwork and sportsmanship. Our sports programs include soccer, basketball, athletics, and more.

Music Band

Discover the joy of music in our school band. Students can learn to play various instruments and participate in musical performances throughout the academic year.

Robotics Club

The school helps learners get hands-on experience in robotics and technology through our Robotics Club. Students learn to build and program robots, participating in regional and national robotics competitions.

Ready to discover your talents and interests? Join an extracurricular activity today!
