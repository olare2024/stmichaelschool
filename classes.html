Classes

Explore our diverse range of classes designed to provide quality education and holistic development for students of all grades.

Play Group

Our Play Group class provides a joyful and stimulating environment for toddlers aged 2-3 years. In this class, we focus on early socialization, basic motor skills, and creative activities to foster a love for learning. Our experienced teachers ensure a safe and caring atmosphere for your child's early development.

Pre-Primary 1 & 2

Pre-Primary education at St. Michael FSJ School is designed for children aged 4-6 years. We follow a play-based curriculum that emphasizes foundational skills in numeracy, literacy, and social interaction. Our goal is to create a supportive and engaging learning environment to prepare students for a smooth transition to formal education.

Grade 1-9

St. Michael FSJ School offers a dynamic learning experience for students in Grade 1-9 through the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) of Kenya. Our curriculum focuses on developing key competencies, critical thinking, and practical skills. We provide a well-rounded education covering languages, sciences, humanities, and extracurricular activities to nurture the holistic growth of each student.

Class Schedule

Our classes follow a well-structured schedule to ensure effective learning and participation in extracurricular activities.
