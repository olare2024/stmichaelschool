Providing Nutritious and Balanced Meals for Students

Meal Plans

At St. Michael School, we understand the importance of providing wholesome and balanced meals to support the well-being and academic success of our students. Our meal plans are designed to offer a variety of nutritious options, accommodating different tastes and dietary needs.

Breakfast Options

  • Whole Grain Cereal with Milk and Fresh Fruits
  • Oatmeal with Toppings: Honey, Raisins, Nuts, and Sliced Apples
  • Yogurt Parfait with Granola and Fresh Fruit Layers

Lunch Choices

  • Sandwich Bar with Whole-Grain Options, Lean Proteins, and Fresh Veggies
  • Vegetable Pasta Salad with a Light Vinaigrette Dressing
  • Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry with Brown Rice or Quinoa

Snack Time

  • Fresh Fruit and Cheese
  • Veggies with Hummus
  • Greek Yogurt with Mixed Berries

Dinner Menu

  • Grilled Chicken or Fish
  • Baked Sweet Potatoes
  • Steamed Vegetables (Broccoli, Cauliflower, Carrots)

Beverage Options

  • Water - Encouraged as the Primary Beverage
  • Milk - Low-Fat or Non-Fat Options
  • 100% Fruit Juice (in Moderation)

Considerations

  • Allergies and Dietary Restrictions: Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions your child may have.
  • Portion Sizes: We provide age-appropriate portion sizes to ensure a balanced diet.
  • Educational Component: We incorporate educational elements to promote healthy eating habits among students.
