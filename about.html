About St. Michael FSJ School

The school was started as part of local community women group programe to support orphans before it got sponsorship by the German organization in partnership with the catholic Sisters organization FSJ

St. Michael FSJ School is dedicated to providing high-quality education in a nurturing and inclusive environment in line with the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). Our mission is to empower learners with knowledge, critical thinking skills,care for the environment, love for the country and values that prepare them for a successful future.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At St. Michael FSJ School, we prioritize academic excellence, character development, and fostering a love for learning. Our dedicated faculty and staff work collaboratively to create a positive and engaging educational experience for all students.
