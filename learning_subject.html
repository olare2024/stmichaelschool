Learning Areas in the CBC Education Cycle

As per the Kenya Basic Education Curriculum Framework 2016, the following are the learning areas at different levels of CBC education:

PRE-PRIMARY 1 AND 2 (EYE LEVEL)

Learning areas at this level are:

  • Mathematical Activities
  • Language Activities
  • Environmental Activities
  • Psychomotor and Creative Activities
  • Religious and Moral Activities

The minimum age for entry into PP1 is 4 years.

LOWER PRIMARY GRADE 1-3 (EYE LEVEL)

Learning areas at this level are:

  • Literacy Activities
  • Indigenous languages/Braille
  • Kiswahili Language Activities/Kenya Sign Language (KSL)
  • English Language Activities
  • Mathematical Activities
  • Religious Education and Life Skills Activities
  • Environmental Activities (Science, Social, and Agriculture)
  • Hygiene and Nutrition Activities
  • Movement and Creative Activities (PE, Music, Art, Craft)
  • Pastoral Program of Instruction

JUNIOR SECONDARY (MIDDLE SCHOOL LEVEL)

Here learners are required to take 12 core subjects namely:

  • English
  • Kiswahili/Kenya Sign Language
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Health Education
  • Pre-Technical and Pre-Career Education
  • Social Studies
  • Business Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Life Skills Education
  • Sports and Physical Education
  • Religious Education (CRE, IRE, HRE)

Learners are additionally required to take a minimum of one and a maximum of two optional subjects from among the following:

  • Visual Arts
  • Home Science
  • Performing Arts
  • Computer Science
  • Kenya Sign Language (KSL)
  • Indigenous Languages
  • Foreign Languages (German, French, Chinese, Arabic)
