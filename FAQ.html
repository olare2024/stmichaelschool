Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the age requirement for admission to St. Michael School?
We admit students to different classes based on age. Please check our admission criteria for specific age requirements for each class.
2. What curriculum does St. Michael FSJ School follow?
St. Michael FSJ School follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) in Kenya, providing a modern and comprehensive education tailored to each student's needs.
3. Are there extracurricular activities offered at St. Michael FSJ School?
Yes, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural programs, to enhance the overall development of our students.
4. How can I apply for admission to St. Michael FSJ School?
You can find the admission process and application forms on our official website. Follow the instructions provided to submit your application.
5. What are the class sizes at St. Michael School?
Our class sizes are designed to ensure effective teacher-student interaction. The specific class sizes may vary, and details can be obtained from the school administration.
6. How does St. Michael FSJ School ensure the safety of its students?
The safety of our students is a top priority. We have strict security measures in place, well-trained staff, and a safe school environment to ensure the well-being of every student.
7. Is there a school uniform policy at St. Michael FSJ School?
Yes, St. Michael School has a school uniform policy. Details about the uniform, including where to purchase it, can be found in our school guidelines.
8. Are scholarships or financial aid programs available?
St. Michael FSJ School is committed to providing quality education. We offer scholarship programs and financial aid to eligible students. Please contact our admissions office for more information.
9. How does St. Michael FSJ School support students with special needs?
Our school is inclusive, and we provide support for students with special needs. We have a dedicated team to assess and cater to the individual needs of each student.
10. How can parents get involved in the St. Michael School community?
Parents are encouraged to be actively involved in their child's education. St. Michael FSJ School organizes regular parent-teacher meetings, events, and volunteer opportunities for parents to engage with the school community.
11. Are meals provided for students at St. Michael School?
Yes, St. Michael FSJ School offers a comprehensive meal program. Our cafeteria provides nutritious and well-balanced meals to learners. We cater to various dietary needs and strive to create a positive dining experience for all.
12. Does St. Michael School provide accommodation for students?
Yes, St. Michael FSJ School currently offers boarding accommodation. The boarding starts from grade 3 to grade 9. Therefore, learners who come from far places can stay in school during the term and enjoy conducive learning opportunities as opposed to day scholars.
13. How is medication managed at St. Michael School?
St. Michael FSJ School has established protocols to ensure the safe administration of medication on the school. Parents and guardians are required to fill out a Medication Authorization Form, providing details about the prescribed medication, dosage, and administration instructions. Our trained staff is responsible for administering medication during school hours. For more information and to access the required forms, please visit the Health Page.
14. Can I schedule a tour of St. Michael FSJ School?
Absolutely! We encourage prospective students and their families to visit our school and experience our educational environment. To schedule a tour, please fill out our online Tour Request Form and Download PDF. Our admissions team will contact you to confirm the tour date and provide additional information, including available tour times and any specific requirements you may have.
15. What is the religious affiliation of St. Michael FSJ School?
St. Michael FSJ School is proud of its Catholic identity. While rooted in faith, we embrace diversity and foster an inclusive environment for students of all faiths. Our goal is to promote understanding, respect, and shared values among our diverse student body.
