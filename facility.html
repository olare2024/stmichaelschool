Our Facilities

Learners at St. Michael School experience a conducive learning environment with our state-of-the-art facilities that support academic and extracurricular activities.

Modern Classrooms

Spacious and well-equipped classrooms designed to enhance the learning experience of our students.

Science Laboratories

Explore the wonders of science in our fully equipped laboratories for practical and hands-on experiments.

Sports Complex

Engage in physical activities and sports in our dedicated sports complex, promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Library and Resource Center

A vast collection of books, digital resources, and study spaces for academic research and self-study.

Technology Hub

Access to the latest technology and computing resources to enhance digital literacy skills.

Arts and Music Studios

Dedicated spaces for creative expression, including art studios and music practice rooms.

Outdoor Learning Spaces

Explore outdoor classrooms and learning spaces for a refreshing and nature-infused educational experience.

Health and Wellness Center

A facility focused on promoting student health and well-being, including medical services and counseling.

Conference and Seminar Halls

Spaces for hosting conferences, seminars, and guest lectures, fostering intellectual discussions.

Cafeteria

A vibrant cafeteria offering nutritious meals and a social space for students to connect during breaks.

Interactive Smart Boards

Engage in dynamic learning with interactive smart boards in classrooms for visual and interactive lessons.

Community Service Center

Participate in community service initiatives through our dedicated center for community outreach.

Toilet Facilities

Modern and well-maintained toilet facilities for the convenience of our students and staff.

Water Facilities

Accessible water facilities to ensure students have a convenient source of clean and safe drinking water.

Computer Labs

Equipped computer labs with the latest technology to enhance computer literacy and programming skills.

Dormitories

Comfortable and secure dormitory facilities for students who reside on the school premises.

Sports Facilities

Specialized sports facilities for various games and athletics, encouraging physical fitness and teamwork.

Robotics Lab

Explore the field of robotics in our specialized lab equipped with the latest robotic technologies.

Discover the richness of our facilities that contribute to a holistic educational experience. Visit us to explore more!
